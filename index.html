Why Study HCI?

Human-Computer Interaction is at the heart of every digital experience. Master the principles that make technology truly usable.

User-Centered Design

Learn to put users at the center of your design process. Understand user needs, behaviors, and motivations through research and testing.

Usability Engineering

Master the systematic approach to creating usable systems. Learn evaluation methods, design guidelines, and iterative design processes.

Research Methods

Discover qualitative and quantitative research methods including user interviews, surveys, A/B testing, and usability studies.

Accessibility Design

Create inclusive experiences for all users. Learn WCAG guidelines, assistive technologies, and universal design principles.

Emerging Technologies

Explore HCI in AR/VR, AI interfaces, voice UI, and other cutting-edge technologies shaping the future of interaction.

Data-Driven Design

Learn to use analytics, heatmaps, and user feedback to make informed design decisions and measure success.
